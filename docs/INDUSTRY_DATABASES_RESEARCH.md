# Industry Databases for Chatbot Integration

## Overview

This document lists publicly available databases and datasets from various industries that can be integrated into your RAG chatbot system. These are organized by industry and include compliance, regulatory, technical, and reference databases.

## Chemical & Environmental Compliance

### 1. **TSCA Chemical Inventory** (EPA)
- **Source:** U.S. Environmental Protection Agency
- **URL:** https://www.epa.gov/tsca-inventory
- **Data Type:** Chemical substances list, CAS numbers, regulatory status
- **Format:** CSV, Excel, API
- **Use Case:** Chemical compliance queries, substance lookups
- **Similar to:** REACH, Prop 65

### 2. **RoHS Database** (EU)
- **Source:** European Union
- **URL:** https://ec.europa.eu/environment/waste/rohs_eee/
- **Data Type:** Restricted substances, exemptions, compliance requirements
- **Format:** PDF, Excel, HTML
- **Use Case:** Electronics compliance, material restrictions
- **Similar to:** REACH Annex XVII

### 3. **WEEE Database** (EU)
- **Source:** European Union
- **URL:** https://ec.europa.eu/environment/waste/weee/
- **Data Type:** Waste electrical and electronic equipment regulations
- **Format:** PDF, HTML
- **Use Case:** Electronics recycling compliance

### 4. **GHS Classification Database**
- **Source:** UN, various national agencies
- **URL:** https://unece.org/transport/dangerous-goods/ghs-rev9-2021
- **Data Type:** Globally Harmonized System of Classification and Labelling
- **Format:** PDF, database
- **Use Case:** Chemical hazard classification, SDS requirements

## Integration Approach

For each database:
1. **Identify data format** (CSV, JSON, PDF, HTML, API)
2. **Create extraction script** (similar to REACH pipeline)
3. **Convert to Markdown** (with metadata frontmatter)
4. **Index in vector database** (using existing DocumentAgent)
5. **Add specialized search tools** (if needed, like CAS number search)
